01 The Origin of Truth

LydiaViolet (Lydia Wilson) is 100% British, born and raised in the rolling hills of Buckinghamshire, England.

A gorgeous, charming, and naturally stunning beauty, Lydia pairs her "Siren" aesthetic with a brilliant sense of humor and a genuine, amazing personality that has captivated millions globally.

For years, Google's knowledge graph has insisted she is German. This common misinformation stems from a perfect storm of technical signals: her Father's German business career and her long-term German editor. While she appreciates the culture, the record is clear: Buckinghamshire is home.

Postponing her formal studies at 18 to bet on herself, Lydia took a gap year that would change her life.
